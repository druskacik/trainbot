About TrainBot

Flying is fast. It is also one of the most carbon-intensive ways to travel. Night trains offer a real alternative — you leave in the evening, sleep on board, and arrive in the morning in another city, no airport required.

TrainBot exists to make night trains easier to book. It tracks fares daily across European Sleeper and Nightjet, and surfaces the lowest prices so you spend less time searching and more time planning.

It covers routes across Western and Central Europe — from Brussels to Barcelona, Vienna to Amsterdam, and more. Search by seat type, set a return window, and get a direct link to book at the source.

No subscriptions, no accounts. Just prices.
