About TrainBot

TrainBot is a one-person project to help people find the best deals on night trains in Europe.

Currently, it covers routes across Western and Central Europe — from Brussels to Prague, Berlin to Paris, Budapest to Zurich, and more. The prices are scraped for 3 months into the future.

TrainBot was made by Robert Druska - feel free to connect on LinkedIn. I'll be happy for any feedback or suggestions for this project!
